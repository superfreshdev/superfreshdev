01/03
One day, after a tough workout, I jumped into an ice-cold shower. At first, it felt brutal — but then I grabbed some amazing-smelling shampoo, closed my eyes, and imagined myself as a fearless Viking from Norway. Cold?
That’s in my blood! Suddenly I started laughing and decided to actually enjoy the freezing water.
02/03
When I stepped out, I couldn’t stop smiling. I felt clean, powerful, and one word popped into my mind: super fresh.
That was the moment I fell in love with this idea. Being super fresh isn’t just about feeling clean — it’s about feeling happy, full of energy, and ready to take on the next challenge of the day.
03/03
A few days later, I decided to turn this feeling into a personal statement. Everything I do, I want it to be super fresh.
Whether it’s pushing myself in sports, developing software, exploring healthy food, or starting new projects — it should bring joy, energy, and something valuable, both to me and to others.
